OUR PHILOSOPHY

Stress doesn’t differentiate!

We realised a ecade back tha as cities got denser and congested a weekend home would no longer be a luxury but a necessity. A weekend home would no longer be just a place to brag about in social circles.

A weekend home would be where you could heal your bruised mind, body and soul. A place where you could pause your worries. A place where you could breathe freely. A place where you opened doors to nature..

For as professionals ourselves we believed - Stress doesn’t differentiate. .

We are not realtors but professionals who love working with nature and creating stress free environments. Our aspiration therefore was that a weekend home should no longer be the preserve of a privileged few but made available to larger diaspora of professionals..

Thus was formed Forever Spring.

Exclusive yet Affordable. Affordable yet discrete. Discrete yet accessible.

We handpick plots in weekend destinations or even virgin destinations just so that a wider set of people can experience nature and the joy of missing out on stressful city living.

No matter what your budget we have a weekend home just for you far we believe stressful living need no longer be a inevitable part of modern day living but a completely avoidable one.

Come experience nature. Come experience Santusthaa.

Worry - free living creates
Happy memories !

Stress perhaps the only thing that beats Inflation in terms of growth. Times where work and home have become synonymous.

A weekend therefore becomes critical not just for you but also your loved ones. For it can be the time to bond, it can be the time to experience togetherness, it can be the time to nourish your heart with the laughter of your young ones.

It can be the time to create life long memories, happy memories away from the hustle bustle of chores, errands and drudgery of modern day living.

The Khopoli Pali Belt

We were among the early few to realise the potential of the otherwise nondescript yet picturesque Khopoli - Pali belt.

Nestled in the ecologically sensitive Western Ghats this township of over 700 homes is just a couple of hours away from India’s Commercial capital - Mumbai and easily accessible from the Mumbai Pune expressway.

While others were selling plots we boldly invested in a weekend home township spread across 3 phases and over 10 buildings.

For as professionals ourselves we had a simple aspiration - Stress doesn’t differentiate. hence a weekend home should no longer be the preserve of a privileged few but made available to a larger diaspora of professionals.

Where weekdays became weekends!

When you are working from home your living spaces are fused.

In such a high pressure environment how does one marry calmness and stress, manage pressure and productivity?

Simple by extending the 2 day weekend into the whole week. Which was what a whole lot of our customers exactly did during the pandemic?

If work and home were fused why not fuse weekdays with weekends.
